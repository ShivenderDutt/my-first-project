About Us

Many years ago, in 1991 to be precise, a young woman, a teacher, a mother, a wife, a daughter, had a dream. A dream to embark upon a journey to help working women leave their little ones at a place where they could learn and play in a safe environment.
Thus was born Prakrit, a daycare centre cum pre-school. The rest, as they say, is history, in the midst of the changing landscape of school education in Odisha, over the last three decades.

About Us

Our Team

FOUNDER'S MESSAGE

Mother’s Public School commenced in 1991 as a preschool in Prakrit with a strength of twenty students. Over the years, Mother’s Public School evolved into a Senior Secondary School with over 2000 students. Originally focused on providing a safe space for working mothers, it pioneered day-boarding in the city. Today, it offers a stimulating environment for pre-schoolers, blending creative learning with practical education. The school’s commitment to nurturing well-rounded students has attracted attention statewide, emphasizing tradition, transferable skills, and holistic development

Warm regards,

Poly Pattnaik

Managing Director’s Message

Dear Students, Parents, and Educators, I am pleased to welcome you to a new academic year filled with opportunities for growth, learning, and transformation. Guided by our mission to provide career-oriented education enriched with moral values and essential life skills, we are dedicated to creating an environment where every student can aspire, learn, and excel.

Director's Message

Somewhere, something incredible is waiting to be known.” — Carl Sagan. Education is not just about learning from textbooks. It is a collaborative effort among children, teachers, and parents. True learning goes beyond classroom lessons; it involves nurturing curiosity and encouraging children to ask questions. At Mothers’ School, we celebrate each child’s uniqueness and aim to instill a lifelong love for learning. We provide a holistic environment where teaching is a pleasure, and learning is a joy.
